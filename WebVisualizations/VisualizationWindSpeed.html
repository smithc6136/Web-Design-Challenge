City Latitude vs. Wind Speed (mph)

Scatterplot:


Discussion of Scatterplot:

This scatterplot compares latitude to wind speed in miles per hour for various cities as of 01/05/2017. Overall we see a slight positive correlation in the data meaning that wind speed generally increases as latitude increases. However, because the correlation is weak we know that latitude is not a great indicator of wind speed.
